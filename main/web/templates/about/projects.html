Sustainable Restaurants

The “Greenhouse Restaurant” concept is a locally sourced fine dining restaurant. All the food that is used in the restaurant comes from these greenhouses and the food waste generated in the restaurant will be composted and used again in the soil. This concept will help to address the issues of sustainability, food waste, and locally sourced food in the restaurant industry.
