Mission and Vision

The aims of the website are to educate the public about sustainability issues that span environmental, social, and economic domains and to encourage the community to become more engaged in sustainability initiatives in Colorado and beyond.

Designed to be a sustainable project, the website will be maintained and expanded by CIS200 students in future semesters to maintain timely and high-quality content.
