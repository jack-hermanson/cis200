History

Emboldened to not only learn about harnessing business for a better world, Colorado State University students in CIS200 decided to actively engage in projects that better the world. During Fall 2021, CIS200 students imagined, designed, and built this website to showcase student-driven projects that highlight business sustainability challenges and solutions. Students agreed that weâ€™re living in a time when fact-based information can be hard to come by, so they prioritized the presentation of evidence-based content on this website, implementing peer review and vetting peer-reviewed sources. Furthermore, by engaging in the process of designing and constructing this website, students were able to gain hands-on experience with the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC), including Scrum, an agile methodology.

Mission and Vision

The aims of the website are to educate the public about sustainability issues that span environmental, social, and economic domains and to encourage the community to become more engaged in sustainability initiatives in Colorado and beyond. Designed to be a sustainable project, the website will be maintained and expanded by CIS200 students in future semesters to maintain timely and high-quality content.
