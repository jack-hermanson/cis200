BizRams Building a Better World​

Information is Power.

Emboldened to not only learn about harnessing business for a better world, Colorado State University students in two sections of CIS200 Business Information Systems decided to actively engage in projects that better the world.

During Fall 2021, CIS200 students imagined, designed, and built this website to showcase student-driven projects that highlight business sustainability challenges and solutions. Students agreed that we’re living in a time when fact-based information can be hard to come by, so they prioritized the presentation of evidence-based content on this website, consulting peer-reviewed sources and collecting primary data through surveys and interviews with experts.

Furthermore, by engaging in the process of designing and constructing this website, students were able to gain hands-on experience with the Systems Development Life Cycle (SDLC), including Scrum, an agile framework.

View student projects by selecting the business sustainability areas of interest (economic, environmental, and social) in the top navigation. Be sure to learn more about the student teams and what they gained from this project experience by visiting Meet the Teams.
