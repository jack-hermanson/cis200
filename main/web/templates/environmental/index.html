Agriculture and Water

According to 303 Magazine, “Over 40% of what we produce in the US [is] thrown away and not used. This is enough to feed everyone who lives in the US and not wasting food could end hunger tomorrow”. This is a large issue in the restaurant industry that needs to be addressed.

Restaurant Sustainability

Food production is putting chemicals into our rivers, sending CO2 into the air we breathe, and destroying the land that our animals live on. While we need food to survive, we have to look at the long-term impacts that production is having on our environment and society as a whole.
