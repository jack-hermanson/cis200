Meet the Teams

To create this website, numerous teams of almost 200 CSU undergraduate business students formed an organization dedicated to researching and sharing evidence-based information about business sustainability challenges and solutions in Colorado and beyond.

The Marketing team designed the website, the Information Systems team built the website, and the Multimedia Projects teams developed video and/or article content for the website. Other teams served in key supporting roles to promote successful completion of the website project: Project Management, Human Resources & Conflict Resolution, and Learning Support.

The Marketing team designs the website, maintains the logo, and promotes the website and student projects.

Marketing

The Information Systems team learns and implements current industry tools and practices to build and maintain this website.

Information Systems

The Multimedia teams research, design, and develop business sustainability video and/or article content for the website.

Multimedia

The Project Management team helps keep various components of the project on track and manages work flow.


Project Management

The Human Resources team helps to promote productivity among students in their project teams, while resolving conflicts as they arise.

The Learning Support team helps with designing and delivering instructional resources and assessments to facilitate the learning process.
