Multimedia

The Multimedia teams designed and developed business sustainability content for the website. They researched business sustainability challenges and business ideas and created videos and/or articles to present their projects.

Multimedia Team Student Feedback

"With the B4BW project, it was great to research topics that we are interested in. I thought it was even better than we were pushed to try to find solutions and ways to think outside of the box."

"Especially after hearing the perspectives of the homeless people we interviewed, we were all given a new perspective on this issue and the necessity to find a more permanent resolution than what exists. We hope to shed light on this pressing issue within our communities and now have a deeper, more personal connection to it."

"What l enjoyed about our project was that our interviewees were so passionate. This project proved to me how many people care about our planet and want to help our world become a better place."

"This was one of my first professional interviews I had ever conducted, so it was a cool experience being able to do that. I really enjoyed getting into the interview and asking some real questions. I found that people are more than willing to talk about their work to offer someone else some insight into what they do."

"I learned a lot about video editing and how difficult it was. I am proud because I know I tried something new and put hours of work into it and was able to do it."

"This project forced me to do something I had never done, which was edit a video. This process, although tedious to learn, ended up being a great addition to my arsenal. Our TA did a great job helping us define what a video needs in order to be a good video, and I will take that lesson with me everywhere now."
