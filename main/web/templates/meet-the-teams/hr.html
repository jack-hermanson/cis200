About Us

As we worked on this project as a team, we strived to find excellence in our studies and, while doing so, reach different goals that we laid out. We believe that incentivizing productive work was the first step to help others achieve success and help motivate students to complete their work and ensure their best work was being put forth. We planned on incentivizing productive work by giving out rewards that were directly attributed to being effective and consistent while also being seen as a leader of the group or, in other words, being the most valuable player of your team. We discovered that the best way to increase efficiency in and between other groups was making sure that we fostered good communication within the groups. Promoting this good communication would make the project seem less daunting and goals more achievable since everyone was working together. We wanted to give all students a way to share their thoughts if they are frustrated or have a different opinion. Their voices would be taken into account within their group and be taken into account through Dr. Serrano.

By structuring our group around fostering communication and helping our peers achieve their goals, we learned how to work efficiently as an HR team while helping others. We, as a team, believe that the steps we took reached every one of our goals and made a positive impact on this project.
