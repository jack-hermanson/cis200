Ecosurf Company Pitch

Solo Project Overview

The bulk of my work for the project went into planning the content I wanted to cover. Going in I wanted to do an original business pitch because I haven't done a project like that yet through college, and it incorporates many aspects of the classes I've taken. However, I've seen many projects like this, and I wanted to make mine different. Many business pitch projects focus on values and core ideas and completely skip the logistics of a business; I wanted to cover both. In brainstorming this every week since I got the greenlight, I would take notes on new business concepts I learn about in my classes and tie them into the project. From weeks of doing this, I had ideas on how to structure a new business from the perspective of finance, supply chain management, management, and marketing. I also had to learn about surfboards to make an idea of a green surfboard and what materials I would use. I had to look at every material and research eco-friendly alternatives, as well as ideas on how to acquire them.

Business Problem

The problem with industry that I’m trying to solve is that soft-top surfboards are made from synthetic, harmful materials, and they only last a couple of years. Alternatives require sourcing from trees, and this presents a different environmental problem.

Proposed Business Solution

I’m proposing a new company, Ecosurf, that will produce and sell sustainable soft-top surfboards that will still incorporate top-of-the-line materials while being sustainably made.

Custom Business Logo

Using Adobe Photoshop, I created a business logo for the company I’m proposing. The inspiration was a recycling sign incorporating ocean waves.​
