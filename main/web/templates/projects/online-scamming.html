Online Scamming

For our project, we created a fun, informational video about unconventional methods of online scamming targeted towards younger generations.

In our video, we cover Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist scams. We also focus on Venmo scams.

The types of Venmo scams we discuss are:

  • Phone Call Scams
  • You Won a Prize Scam
  • A Friend Needs Help Scam
  • "Suggar Mommy" or "Sugar Daddy" Scam

Importantly, we discuss several ways to identify and avoid online scams.
