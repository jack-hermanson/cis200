BizRams Building a Better World

Emboldened to not only learn about harnessing business for a better world, Colorado State University students decided to actively engage in projects that better the world.

During Fall 2021, two sections of CIS200 Business Information Systems students imagined, designed, and built this website to showcase student-driven projects centered on business sustainability challenges and solutions.

Through their projects, students learned an important lesson, one that underscores a core tenet of this website: information is power.

With rising concerns about online misinformation and disinformation, students aspired to harness the power of information to better the world and unite our communities. Prioritizing the presentation of evidence-based content on this website, teams consulted peer-reviewed sources and collected primary data through surveys, interviews with experts, and field observations, with the end goal of sharing their projects with the community.

View student projects by selecting the business sustainability areas of interest (economic, environmental, and social) in the top navigation. Be sure to learn more about the student teams and what they gained from this project experience by visiting Meet the Teams.
