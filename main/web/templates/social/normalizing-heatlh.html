Normalizing Health

Welcome to the Normalizing Health page.

This video portrays the harsh, unknown truths regarding the realties of living an obese lifestyle. Not only does this video portray the debilitating effects that obesity has on the body (both mentally and physically), it also uncovers the reality that obesity does not just affect a person’s body, it also takes a negative toll on the economy as well as society. Throughout this video, viewers are bombarded with facts from scholarly sources that aid in the explanation of why obesity is becoming such a prevalent issue within today’s society. From The World Health Organization to the Pakistan Journal of Medical Science, these scholarly sources are proved to have accurate facts that aid in providing the jarring truths of living an obese lifestyle. There have been many surveys that show how obese individuals feel about their situation and how they would prefer to be a healthy weight and poor than obese and a multimillionare. Additionally, obese business people are faced with discrimination and prejudice in the work plae. This reality can be disheartening for the future of business for a better world.

However, this video is not only to inform viewers about the harm obesity brings to both the human body and the environment, this video also provides solutions (including existing solutions). For example, a solution shown within the video is how Google is implementing certain regulations to provide their employees with a less stressful work environment.

Overall, this video was created in order to preach to viewers that although obesity is a rising epidemic within today’s society, it is preventable and/or not a terminal disease. Through gaining education and knowledge on not only how to prevent obesity, but how to overcome obesity as well, the decrease in obesity rates within today’s society are sure to come!
